CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Picture Name Description
Juan Crisostomo Ibaara y Magsalin
  • The story's protagonist
  • a young Filipino who returns to the Philippines from Europe to seek justice for his father's death and to make social reforms.
Maria Clara
  • The daughter of Kapitan Tiago.
  • she is in a romantic relationship with Crisostomo Ibarra and embodies purity and innocence.
Elias
  • Crisostomo Ibarra's companion.
  • He is a mysterious and courageous man. He adores the Philippines and desires vengeance on the tyrannical Spanish regime.
Kaptian Tiago
  • A wealthy Filipino who arranges social gatherings and activities at his home on a regular basis.
  • He has friendships with both Ibarra and the friars.
Padre Damaso
  • He is the real father of Maria clara.
  • He is opposed of the marriage between Ibarra and Maria Clara.
Padre Salvi
  • Another unscrupulous and scheming priest, the curate of San Diego.
  • He is an adversary of Padre Damaso.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the Father of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • His death drives his son's homecoming and desire for justice.
Pilosopo Tasyo(Don Anastacio)
  • He is an old scholar living in San Diego.
  • he became an adviser for several individuals in town due to his unorthodox ideas.
Sisa
  • She is the mother of both Basilio and Crispin.
  • She became insane when both of her sons went missing.
Crispin
  • He is the second son of Sisa.
  • He, together with his brother was accused of stealing in the San Diego Church.
  • He was beaten then was presumed dead.
Basilio
  • He is the first son of Sisa.
  • He, together with his brother was accused of stealing in the San Diego Church.
  • He, unlike his brother, was able to escape the punishment.
Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • She is a hilarious Filipina who tries to be European in manners and looks.
  • She, Together with her Husband, associate themselves with the influential figures to gain popularity.
Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • Is the Husband of Donya Victorina de Espadaña.
  • He, Together with his wife, associate themselves with the influential figures to gain popularity.
Donya Consolacion
  • She is the wicked and cruel wife of the town's Police Chief.
Alperes
  • He is the town's cruel police chief.
  • He is being told what to do by Donya Consolacion
Tiya Isabel
  • She is devoted Ibarra family member.
  • She plays a maternal role in Crisostomo's life.
