Lee Ha Yi

Background

Lee Hi, born Lee Ha-yi on September 23, 1996, is a South Korean singer-songwriter known for her soulful vocals and unique musical style. She rose to prominence as a contestant on the first season of the South Korean reality competition show "K-pop Star" in 2011, where she finished as the runner-up. Following her success on the show, Lee Hi debuted under YG Entertainment with the single "1, 2, 3, 4" in 2012, which became a commercial hit. Throughout her career, she has released several successful albums and singles, establishing herself as one of the prominent solo artists in the K-pop industry. Lee Hi is celebrated for her versatility, often exploring different genres such as R&B, jazz, and pop in her music.
